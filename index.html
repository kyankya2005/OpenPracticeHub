About OpenPracticeHub

OpenPracticeHub was created to help beginners overcome the fear of making their first open-source contribution. We believe that everyone should have a safe space to practice GitHub collaboration without the pressure of working on critical projects.

Our mission is to build confidence in new developers by providing real, meaningful projects where they can learn, experiment, and grow their skills alongside a supportive community.

üéØ Our Mission

"To empower every beginner to become a confident open-source contributor through practice, learning, and community support - while keeping real projects safe from spam."
